About Berkshire Hathaway

Berkshire Hathaway Inc. is an American multinational conglomerate corporation based in Omaha, Nebraska, United States. Founded by business magnate Warren Buffett, it has since grown into one of the largest conglomerate companies in the world. The company has significant operations in the insurance, energy, and retail sectors, among others.
