About Berkshire Hathaway

Berkshire Hathaway Inc. is a multinational conglomerate holding company headquartered in Omaha, Nebraska, United States. It was founded by Warren Buffett, who is the chairman and CEO. The company owns a diverse range of businesses and investments.

For more detailed information, visit our official website at www.berkshirehathaway.com.
